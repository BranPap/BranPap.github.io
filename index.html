I am a first-year PhD student at Stanford University. I hope to focus my research on sociolinguistics and the linguistic constructions of personae in contemporary popular music. I also hope to expand into the study of linguistic perception of singing, focusing on misheard lyrics and how our social perceptions of musical artsists condition what we mishear.

I completed my Undergraduate degree in Linguistics at the University of Edinburgh in 2020. During my time there, I served as the First Year Representative, Social Secretary, and President of the student Lingusitics Society. I also served as the National Chair of the Undergraduate Linguistics Association of Britain.
